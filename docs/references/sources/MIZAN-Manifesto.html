The Philosophy

Why we call it MIZAN.

The MIZAN Project is Hijra's Financing Origination System — the digital backbone for every financing decision at Hijra as a modern digital bank.

Mīzān (ميزان) is Arabic for "the scales." It is a reminder — and a discipline — that every financing decision is an act of balance:

balance between risk and return;
balance between speed and prudence;
balance between gain and loss.

Our hope: that MIZAN becomes our effort to deliver the best financing experience possible — for every customer, and for the entire Hijra ecosystem.

The Concept

So, what is MIZAN?

MIZAN — Arabic for "the scales of justice" — is Hijra's centralized Financing Origination System, where the entire financing lifecycle lives: from incoming application through disbursement and beyond.

Think of MIZAN as a control tower: one place that sees every application, every stage, every team — and helps every person make a better decision.

Not just a workflow tool. MIZAN is a thinking system for our financing team.

What lives inside MIZAN:

→A centralized pipeline for all financing applications across every akad type.
→An AI assistant for 5C+1S analysis, cash flow review, and red-flag detection.
→End-to-end management of debtors and collateral in one integrated view.
→Digital committee meetings — voting, minutes, and decisions cleanly recorded.
→Portfolio monitoring post-disbursement, with early NPL warnings.
→A full audit trail for compliance and regulators.
● The Concept 05 / 12
How It Works

Five stages. One clear flow.

Every financing application moves through five standardized stages. Each stage has a clear output, a clear owner, and a measurable SLA.

1
Document Submission
Application intake & completeness check
2
Legal & SLIK
BI Checking, legal review, and compliance
3
Feasibility
Due diligence, 5C+1S analysis, collateral appraisal
4
Risk Review
Risk team recommendation and mitigation
5
Committee Decision
Final decision, voting, and disbursement

Why this matters: everyone at Hijra knows exactly where an application stands, who is holding the ball, and when a decision can be expected. No more "let me check on that first…"

● How It Works 06 / 12
Key Features

Not just a database. MIZAN thinks with you.

✦

AI-Assisted Analysis

AI drafts 5C+1S analyses, flags red flags in financial statements, and offers a second opinion — but the final decision stays with the analyst.

◧

Visual Kanban Pipeline

Drag and drop applications between stages. Spot bottlenecks instantly. Prioritize what's urgent without weekly status meetings.

☪

5C+1S Framework

Character, Capacity, Capital, Condition, Collateral — plus Syariah. A standard framework so every application is evaluated at the same depth.

⚖

Digital Committee

Committee sessions with ready-to-read MUAP/RSK documents, real-time voting, and automatic minutes. Every decision recorded and auditable.

⟲

Complete Audit Trail

Every change, approval, and comment captured with timestamp and user. Ready for OJK or internal audit at a moment's notice.

◐

Portfolio Monitoring

After disbursement, MIZAN tracks collectibility, outstanding balances, and watch lists — flagging risks before they turn into NPLs.

A day with MIZAN.
